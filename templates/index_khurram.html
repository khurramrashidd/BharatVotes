About Election Commission of India

The Election Commission of India is an autonomous constitutional authority responsible for administering Union and State election processes in India. Our mission is to ensure free, fair, and transparent elections for a strong democracy.

Established under the Constitution of India, the ECI is a permanent and independent body that supervises the largest democratic exercise in the world.

Our Commitment to Democracy

We are dedicated to enhancing electoral processes through innovation and technology

Our Mission

To conduct free and fair elections, ensuring maximum participation of voters and building trust in the electoral process. We aim to be a leader in electoral management, promoting democratic values.

Key Objectives

  • Ensure every eligible citizen has the right to vote
  • Enhance transparency and integrity of elections
  • Leverage technology for accessible and secure voting
  • Educate voters about electoral processes
